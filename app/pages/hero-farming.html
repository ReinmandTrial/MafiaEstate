Hero Farming

After unlocking the Heroes (by opening chests or buying directly from the store), users can have heroes train in Hero farming mode prepare for the upcoming expeditions next. In Hero farming mode, users will be able to receive rewards of ALIX tokens with APY depending on the character.

The reward distributed in Hero Farming is 150,000,000 ALIX tokens (30% of total supply). The reward will be distributed for 1 year and 10% off every 30 days.

How to Farming?

Step 1: You need to go to the Shop to buy Key

Step 2:Go to "Open Chest" and use the key to open Chest. After that, the chest will randomly appear into 1 of 5 Heroes.

Step 3:Go to Hero Farming and select the farm corresponding to the type of Hero you have.

Step 4:Stake and confirm

Reward

70 ALIX/block in the first 15 days

23 ALIX/block for the next 180 days
