How to Play

  • In order to join Hero Fighting mode, you have to own at least 1 character
  • Once you have the hero, access the hero fighting function and choose a monster to let your hero fight against.
  • Tip: Choose the right opponent (monster) to increase your opportunity to receive the corresponding reward.

NOTE:

  • You only get the reward when you win, if you lose you will not receive the reward and lose your battle turn.
  • You can choose any hero of you to fight against any monster to receive the rewards
